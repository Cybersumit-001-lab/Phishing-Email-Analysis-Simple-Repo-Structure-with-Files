Phishing Indicators Summary


1	Spoofed sender domain           	“access-accsecurity.com” pretending to be Microsoft
2	Reply-To Gmail address	          Leads directly to attacker’s mailbox
3	SPF/DKIM/DMARC failed	            Authentication not verified
4	Mismatched URL                  	Link points to fake login domain
5	Urgent or fear tone              	Forces quick user action
6	Tracking pixel	                  Confirms when victim opens email
7	Grammar/style inconsistencies	    Not professional or standard Microsoft wording
8	Suspicious IP	                    Hosted on non-Microsoft infrastructure
